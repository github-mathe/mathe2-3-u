\Aufgabe[e]{Matrix rank}{
Determine the rank of the matrix
$$
\boldsymbol C =
\left[\begin{array}{rrrrr}
3 &  8 & 6 & -4 & -1 \\
0 &  0 & 1 &  1 &  3 \\
0 &  0 & 0 &  0 &  4 \\
0 & -7 & 8 &  1 &  1 \\
0 &  0 & 0 & -7 &  4 
\end{array}\right]\,.
$$

}

\Loesung{
Permute the rows as
$$
\begin{array}{rrrrr||l}
3 &     8 &     6 &    -4 &    -1 \\
0 &     0 &     1 &     1 &     3 &\text{ 4th row}\\
0 &     0 &     0 &     0 &     4 &\text{ 2nd row}\\
0 &    -7 &     8 &     1 &     1 &\text{ 5th row}\\
0 &     0 &     0 &    -7 &     4 &\text{ 3rd row}\\
\hline
\end{array}\quad\rightarrow\quad
% 
\begin{array}{rrrrr||}
3 &     8 &     6 &    -4 &    -1 \\
0 &    -7 &     8 &     1 &     1 \\
0 &     0 &     1 &     1 &     3 \\
0 &     0 &     0 &    -7 &     4 \\
0 &     0 &     0 &     0 &     4 \\
\hline
\end{array}\,.
$$

This yields $\operatorname{rank}(\boldsymbol C) = 5$.

}
