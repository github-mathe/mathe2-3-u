\Aufgabe[e]{Matrix rank}{
Calculate the rank of the matrix
$$
\boldsymbol B =
\left[\begin{array}{rrr}
 1 & -2 & 0\\
 1 &  1 & 1\\
-1 & -1 & 4\\
 3 &  4 & 3
\end{array}\right]\,.
$$

}

\Loesung{
The Gaussian algorithm is applied as
$$
\begin{array}{rrr||}
 1 & -2 & 0\\
 1 &  1 & 1\\
-1 & -1 & 4\\
 3 &  4 & 3\\
\hline
\end{array} \quad \rightarrow \quad
%
\begin{array}{rrr||}
 1 & -2 & 0\\
 0 &  3 & 1\\
 0 & -3 & 4\\
 0 & 10 & 3\\
\hline
\end{array} \quad \rightarrow \quad
%
\begin{array}{rrr||}
 1 & -2 & 0\\
 0 &  3 & 1\\
 0 &  0 & 5\\
 0 &  0 & 3-\frac{10}{3}\\
\hline
\end{array}
$$

$$
\rightarrow \quad
%
\begin{array}{rrr||}
 1 & -2 & 0\\
 0 &  3 & 1\\
 0 &  0 & 5\\
 0 &  0 & 0\\
\hline
\end{array}
$$

This yields $\operatorname{rank}(\boldsymbol B) = 3$.

}
